Temperature

What It Is:
The warmth or coolness of the environment.
Why It's Important:
Most plants thrive between 60째F and 75째F (15째C to 24째C).
How to Maintain It:
  • Avoid Drafts: Keep plants away from cold windows and hot heaters.
    •
  • Stable Environment: Try to keep the temperature consistent.

Pros:

  • Optimal Growth: Correct temperature ranges promote ideal growth conditions, encouraging plants to thrive.
  • Flowering and Fruiting: Certain temperature ranges are necessary for plants to flower and produce fruit, leading to better yields.
  • Photosynthesis Efficiency: Ideal temperatures enhance the efficiency of photosynthesis, which is crucial for plant growth and development.
  • Dormancy Regulation: Temperature fluctuations help regulate plant dormancy cycles, ensuring proper rest periods and vigorous growth phases.

    • Cons:

  • Heat Stress:Excessively high temperatures can lead to heat stress, causing wilting, leaf scorch, and reduced productivity.
  • Cold Damage:Frost and freezing temperatures can damage plant tissues, leading to stunted growth or even plant death.
  • Delayed Germination: Temperature extremes can delay or inhibit seed germination, affecting the overall growth cycle.
  • Pest and Disease Susceptibility: Unfavorable temperatures can weaken plants, making them more susceptible to pests and diseases.
